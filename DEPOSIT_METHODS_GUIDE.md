## 🚀 How to Use the Deposit Feature

### Step 1: Login to Your Account
Navigate to the login page and sign in with your credentials.

### Step 2: Go to Wallet
Click on **"Wallet"** in the sidebar navigation.

### Step 3: Click Deposit Button
Click the **green "Deposit"** button in the top right corner.

### Step 4: Select Currency
Choose the cryptocurrency or currency you want to deposit:
- USDT (Tether)
- BTC (Bitcoin)
- ETH (Ethereum)
- BNB (Binance Coin)
- USD (Fiat)

### Step 5: Choose Payment Method
Select your preferred payment method from the available options.

### Step 6: Enter Amount
Enter the amount you want to deposit:
- Check the minimum and maximum limits for your chosen method
- The form will validate the amount before submission

### Step 7: View Payment Details
Payment details are automatically displayed:
- **For OPay**: Account name and phone number (copy with one click)
- **For Crypto**: Wallet address (copy with one click)
- **For Bank Transfer**: Details provided after amount confirmation

### Step 8: Make the Payment
- **OPay**: Send money directly using OPay mobile app
- **Crypto**: Send from your wallet to the provided address
- **Card**: Enter card details in the form
- **Bank**: Use the provided account details

### Step 9: Optional - Add Transaction Hash
For cryptocurrency deposits, you can optionally paste the transaction hash for faster confirmation.

### Step 10: Complete Deposit
Click **"Complete Deposit"** button to submit.

## 💡 Tips for Successful Deposits

### For OPay Deposits:
1. Ensure you have sufficient balance in your OPay account
2. Use exact amount if possible
3. Keep the phone number handy during transfer
4. Wait for confirmation message

### For Cryptocurrency Deposits:
1. **Double-check wallet addresses** before sending
2. Use correct network for USDT (TRC20 or BEP20)
3. Paste transaction hash for faster confirmation
4. Wait for required confirmations on blockchain

### For Card Deposits:
1. Ensure 3D Secure is enabled on your card
2. Use a valid card with sufficient balance
3. Check for daily transaction limits

### For Bank Transfers:
1. Verify account details before transfer
2. Use reference number if provided
3. Allow 1-3 business days for processing
4. Keep payment receipt

## ❓ FAQ

### How long does it take for my deposit to be confirmed?
- **OPay**: 5-30 minutes
- **Crypto**: 10-60 minutes (depending on network)
- **Card**: 1-3 minutes
- **Bank**: 1-3 business days

### Is there a deposit fee?
- OPay has a small fee (included in the amount)
- Cryptocurrency fees are network-dependent
- Card deposits may have processing fees
- Direct deposits have no additional fees

### Can I cancel a pending deposit?
- OPay: Contact support if payment not yet made
- Crypto: Cannot cancel once sent
- Card: May be possible if not processed yet
- Bank: Contact support immediately

### What's the minimum deposit amount?
- Varies by method (see above)
- OPay: Minimum $100
- Crypto: Varies by coin
- Card: Minimum $50

### What currencies can I deposit?
- USDT (Tether)
- BTC (Bitcoin)
- ETH (Ethereum)
- BNB (Binance Coin)
- USD (Fiat)

### My deposit is still pending. What should I do?
1. Wait 24 hours for admin verification
2. Check your transaction history
3. Verify payment was sent correctly
4. Contact support with your deposit ID
