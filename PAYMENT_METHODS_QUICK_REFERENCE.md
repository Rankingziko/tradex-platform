## 📋 Deposit Process Steps

1. **Login** to your TRADEX account
2. **Go to Wallet** page
3. **Click Deposit** button
4. **Select Currency** (USDT, BTC, ETH, BNB, USD)
5. **Choose Payment Method** from 8 options
6. **View Payment Details** (automatically displayed)
7. **Copy Address/Account** (one-click copy)
8. **Enter Amount** to deposit
9. **(Optional) Add Transaction Hash** for crypto
10. **Click Complete Deposit**
11. **Make Payment** via your chosen method
12. **Wait for Confirmation** (admin will verify within 24h)
